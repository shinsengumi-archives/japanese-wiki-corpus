Shunkai (俊海)

Shunkai (date of birth and death unknown) was a Shingon sect priest who lived from the end of the Heian period to the early Kamakura period. His father was FUJIWARA no Toshitada, and FUJIWARA no Toshinari was his brother. His children included Jakuren, who was known as a priest and waka poet. He was Ajari at the Daigo-ji Temple.
