Eno Jusshu (猿翁十種)

Eno Jusshu refers to the specialty plays of Ennosuke ICHIKAWA of Omodakaya selected by Ennosuke the third in 1964.

Ten dance dramas which were created and performed best by his grandfather, Eno ICHIKAWA, were selected.

Akutaro (a bad boy) (kabuki)

Kurotsuka (kabuki)

Koyamonogurui

Kokaji

Koma (kabuki)

Ninin Sambaso

Nomitori Otoko (the man catching fleas)

Hanami Yakko (Fellow enjoying the blossoms)

Yoi Yakko (Drunk fellow)

Yoshinoyama michiyuki (travel to Mt. Yoshino)
