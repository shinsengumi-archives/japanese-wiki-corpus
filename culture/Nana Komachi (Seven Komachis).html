Nana Komachi (Seven Komachis) (七小町)

Nanakomachi is a generic term for seven Yokyoku (Noh songs) dealing with ONO no Komachi. It includes following yokyoku.

Sekidera Komachi (Komachi at Seki-dera Temple)
Omu Komachi (Komachi's Parrot-Answer Poem)
Sotoba Komachi (Komachi at the Gravepost)
Kayoi Komachi (Komachi visiting)
Soshiarai Komachi (Komachi Washing the Manuscript)
Amagoi Komachi (Takayasu Komachi) (Komachi Praying for Rain)
Kiyomizu Komachi
