Mineyama Prefecture (峰山県)

Mineyama Prefecture was established in 1871 through Haihan-chiken (abolition of feudal domains and establishment of prefectures). It was merged into Toyooka Prefecture and disappeared. The Mineyama Prefectural area was established in the area of present day Kyotango City.

History
July 14, 1871: Mineyama Domain was inaugurated as Mineyama Prefecture through Haihan-chiken.

November 22, 1871: It was merged into Toyooka Prefecture. August 22, 1876: It was absorbed into Kyoto Prefecture with the abolition of Toyooka Prefecture.
