Imperial Prince Arisugawa-no-miya Tsunahito (有栖川宮韶仁親王)

Arisugawa-no-miya Imperial Prince Tsunahito (January 29, 1785 - April 4, 1845) was a member of the Imperial family who lived in the Edo period. He was the seventh family head of the Arisugawa-no-miya family. His father was Imperial Prince Arisugawa-no-miya Orihito. His children include Imperial Prince Arisugawa-no-miya Takahito and Kinzumi SAIONJI.
