Imperial Prince Fushiminomiya Kunitaka (伏見宮邦高親王)

Imperial Prince Fushiminomiya Kunitaka (March 8, 1456 - April 24, 1532) was an Imperial family member during the late Muromachi period. He was Shikibukyo (chief of the Ministry of Ceremonies).
The fifth family head of the Fushiminomiya family
The first Prince of Imperial Prince Fushiminomiya Sadatsune
His mother was Shigeari NIWATA's daughter, MINAMOTO no Mitsuko.

He had a coming-of-age ceremony in 1474 and received the title of Imperial Prince. He entered into the priesthood in 1516.

His child was Imperial Prince Fushiminomiya Sadaatsu.
