Imperial Prince Fushiminomiya Kunimichi (伏見宮邦道親王)

Imperial Prince Fushiminomiya Kunimichi (1641 - September 1, 1654) was a member of the Imperial family in the early Edo period. He was the twelfth head of the Fushiminomiya family. His father was Imperial Prince Fushiminomiya Sadakiyo who was the tenth head of the Fushiminomiya family.

In 1649, he was given the title of Imperial Prince. In 1651, he experienced genpuku (a coming-of-age celebration for boys).

He had no children.
