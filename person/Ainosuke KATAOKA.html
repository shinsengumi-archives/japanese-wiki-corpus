KATAOKA Ainosuke (片岡愛之助)

Ainosuke KATAOKA is a kabuki actor.
His family stage name is 'Matsushimaya.'
The current holder of the name is the sixth Ainosuke KATAOKA.

Ainosuke KATAOKA (the first)

He was the biological child of Nizaemon KATAOKA (the seventh).

Ainosuke KATAOKA (the second)

He succeeded the name of Chodayu KATAOKA (the fourth) later.

Ainosuke KATAOKA (the third)

He was the pupil of Nizaemon KATAOKA (the eighth).

Ainosuke KATAOKA (the sixth)

He was the adopted child of Hidetaro KATAOKA (the second).
