TAKATSUKASA Fuyutsune (鷹司冬経)

Fuyutsune TAKATSUKASA (1283 - July 17, 1319) was a kugyo (court noble) who lived at the end of the Kamakura period. His father was Kanetada TAKATSUKASA. His mother was the daughter of Motohira KONOE. His older brother was Fuyuhira TAKATSUKASA. He adopted his younger brother, Motonori TAKATSUKASA. Fuyutsune was Shonii Dainagon (Senior Second Rank, Major Counselor).

Brief Personal History

Fuyutsune was born as the second son of Kanetada TAKATSUKASA in 1283. On January 22, 1295, he received the rank of Junior Third Rank. He died in 1319.
