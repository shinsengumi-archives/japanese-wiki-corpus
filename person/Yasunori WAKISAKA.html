WAKISAKA Yasunori (脇坂安教)

Yasunori WAKISAKA (1762 - August 18, 1781) was a successor of Tatsuno Domain of Harima Province. He was the first son of the seventh lord, Yasuchika WAKISAKA. His mother was a daughter of Yoshimasa UEDA. He was Jugoinoge (Junior Fifth Rank, Lower Grade) Omi no kuni no kami (Governor of Omi Province).

He was born as a legitimate son and the successor of Tatsuno Domain, was granted an audience with Ieharu TOKUGAWA in 1777, and was appointed to Jugoinoge. He however died in 1781 at the age of 20 before taking over the family. As a replacement, his younger brother Yasutada WAKISAKA became the legitimate son as the successor.
