KATAOKA Hidetaro (片岡秀太郎)

Hidetaro KATAOKA is a Kabuki (traditional drama performed by male actors) actor. The stage family name is Matsushimaya. Current Hidetaro KATAOKA is the second. The Jomon (family crest) is Nanatsu-wari Maru ni Ni-hiki (literally, seven percents with two lines and a circle).

Hidetaro KATAOKA (the first)

He later became Nizaemon KATAOKA (the 11th).

Hidetaro KATAOKA (the second)

He is the second son of Nizaemon KATAOKA (the 13th).
