SAWAMURA Tojuro (澤村藤十郎)

Tojuro SAWAMURA is a professional name of Kabuki actors. Its stage family name is Kinokuniya (Kabuki). Its Jomon (family crest) is sasa-rindo (bamboo grass and gentians).

Tojuro SAWAMURA the First
His place of origin is unknown.

His record of succession to professional names is unknown.

Tojuro SAWAMURA the Second
He was the second son of Sojuro SAWAMURA the Eighth, 1943 -
He is the present head of the family.
