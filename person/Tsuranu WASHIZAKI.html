WASHIZAKI Tsuranu (鷲崎連)

Tsuranu WASHIZAKI (dates of birth and death unknown) was a Hira Taishi (Regimental Soldier) of Shinsengumi (a group who guarded Kyoto during the end of Tokugawa Shogunate).

On August 20, 1864, he participated in the Kinmon Incident (Conspiracy of Hamaguri-gomon Gate).

Because his previous and subsequent information are unknown, he might be a fictitious character such as Kaigyo TENICHIBO.
