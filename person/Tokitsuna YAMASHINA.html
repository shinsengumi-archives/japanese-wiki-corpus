YAMASHINA Tokitsuna (山科言綱)

Tokitsuna YAMASHINA (May, 4, 1486 - October 3, 1530) was a court noble during the late Muromachi period.
Junii (Junior Second Rank), Gon Dainagon (a provisional chief councilor of state)
Tokikuni YAMASHIA was his father and Tokitsugu YAMASHINA was his son. His lawful wife was the older sister of Jukei-ni, who was the lawful wife of Ujichika IMAGAWA.
