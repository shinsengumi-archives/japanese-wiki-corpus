OKUZAWA Eisuke (奥沢栄助)

Eisuke OKUZAWA (the date of birth unknown - July 8, 1864) was a member of Shinsengumi. Okuzawa was a corporal. It is unknown from which clan he was from.

When the Ikeda-ya incident happened, Okizawa, who belonged to the troops led by Isami KONDO of Shinsengumi, rushed into Ikeda-ya with them, was seriously injured, and died at a stationing facility of Shinsengumi. Aizu clan gave a reward of 20 ryo to Okuzawa.

Okuzawa's grave is located in Mibu-dera Temple.
