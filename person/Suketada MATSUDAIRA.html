MATSUDAIRA Suketada (松平資尹)

Suketada MATSUDAIRA was the second lord of the Miyazu Domain, Tango Province. He was the fifth generation of the Honjo Matsudaira family.

On May 11, 1746, he was born in Edo as the seventh son of Tadayori SAKAI, the fifth lord of the Shonai Domain, Dewa Province. On September 17, 1761, he was adopted by Sukemasa MATSUDAIRA, the first lord of the Miyazu Domain, succeeded to the family head, and became the second lord of the Domain due to Sukemasa's retirement on December 22.

He died in Edo on September 8, 1765. He was 20 years old. His adopted child Suketsugu MATSUDAIRA succeeded to the post.
