NAKAMURA Shibajaku (中村芝雀)

Shibajaku NAKAMURA is a professional name used by several Kabuki actors. The family's stage name is Kyoya. The current holder of the name is the seventh generation Shibajaku NAKAMURA. The family crest is called Kyoya musubi.
