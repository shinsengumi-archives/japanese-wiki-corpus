SAKURAMA Kyusen (櫻間弓川)

Kyusen SAKURAMA (May 18, 1889 - March 1, 1957) was a Noh actor of the Konparu school of shite-kata (lead actors). His real name was Kintaro SAKURAMA.

He was the second son of Banma SAKURAMA. Michio SAKURAMA was his cousin. He was a leading Noh actor of the Konparu school in the Taisho and Showa periods. Kinataro SAKURAMA was his eldest son.
