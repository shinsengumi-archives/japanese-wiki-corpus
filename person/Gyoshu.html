Gyoshu (行修)

Gyoshu (dates of birth and death unknown) was a priest of Daian-ji Temple in the Nara period.

Summary

Between 770 and 781, he served as 大寺主 daihoshi at Daian-ji Temple. It is said that his name was written in Okugaki (postscript) of the first, third and seventh volumes of "Daihokojuringyo."
