SAWAMURA Sonosuke (澤村宗之助)

Sonosuke SAWAMURA is a name which has been used by some Kabuki (a traditional form of drama and music performed by male actors) actors. The current Sonosuke is the third generation.

Sonosuke SAWAMURA the First
He was the second son of Tosshi SAWAMURA the Seventh.

Sonosuke SAWAMURA the Second
He was the eldest son of the first generation.

Sonosuke SAWAMURA the Third
He was a pupil of Sojuro SAWAMURA the Ninth.
