25 Sacred Sites of Floral Temples in the Kansai Region (関西花の寺二十五霊場)

The 25 Sacred Sites of Floral Temples in the Kansai Region is a pilgrimage covering 25 temples famous for seasonal flowers across the six prefectures of Osaka, Hyogo, Kyoto, Shiga, Nara and Wakayama. The pilgrimage was established in order to use the beautiful flowers and autumn leaves to strengthen the relationship that believers have with the temples as well as make life and society bright and prosperous.
