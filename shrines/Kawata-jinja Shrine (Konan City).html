Kawata-jinja Shrine (Konan City) (川田神社 (湖南市))

Kawata-jinja Shrine is a Shinto shrine located in Kosei-cho, Konan City, Shiga Prefecture. It is a Shikinaisha ronja (a Shinto shrine considered to be a descendant of shrines listed in Engishiki laws).

The enshrined deity

Amenokoyane no mikoto

History
The first enshrinement date is unknown. It is a shrine considered to be a descendant of shrines registered in the list of deities under Engishiki laws.

The crest for the shrine
Mitsudomoe

Festival

Reisai, May 1
