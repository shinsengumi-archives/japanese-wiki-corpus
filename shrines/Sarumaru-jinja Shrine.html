Sarumaru-jinja Shrine (猿丸神社)

Sarumaru-jinja Shrine is located in Ujitawara-cho, Tsuzuki-gun of Kyoto Prefecture. This shrine was ranked as Mukaku-sha (an ungraded shrine) in the old shrine classification system.

Enshrined deity
Sarumaru Dayu

History
It is unknown when this shrine was founded, but according to legend, the main building was rebuilt in 1645.

Address
44 Zenjoji-tsumakitani, Ujitawara-cho, Tsuzuki-gun, Kyoto Prefecture

Fair
On the 13th of each month
