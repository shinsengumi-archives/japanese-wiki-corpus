Hanamikoji-dori Street (花見小路通)

Hanamikoji-dori Street is a street running east-west through Kyoto City. Hanamikoji-dori Street spans a length of about 1 km from Sanjo-dori Street at its north end to Yasui Kitamon-dori Street at its south end, and is the main street passing through the central zone of Gion.

The section of Hanamikoji-dori Street south of Shijo-dori Street started to develop during the Meiji period with tea rooms and restaurants lining the road.
