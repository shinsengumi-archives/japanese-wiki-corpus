Chiba no kunimiyatsuko (千葉国造)

Chiba no kunimiyatsuko 千葉国造 was kuninomiyatsuko (local ruling families in ancient Japan) ruled the middle south of Kazusa Province. "Manyoshu" (the first major anthology of early Japanese poetry) describes it as 知波国造.

Clan

Ookisaibenoatae clan

Base

Chiba Gun, Shimousa Province. The most part of the present Chiba City, Narashino City, the east part of Funabashi City, the west part of Yachiyo City.

Local tutelar deity

Unknown.

Descendants

OOKISAIBE no Yoshihito: A person during the Heian period
