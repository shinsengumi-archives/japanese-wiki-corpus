Motosu no kunimiyatsuko (本巣国造)

Motosu no kunimiyatsuko (also known as Motosukokuzo) was kuninomiyatsuko (local ruling families in ancient Japan) ruled the central west of Mino Province.

Ancestor

According to "Kojiki," Yatsuriirihikonomiko, a son of Hikoimasuo, was the ancestor.

Clan

The Minomotosu clan
Kabane was Atai. The clan was descended from the same line as the Nagahatabe clan.

Base

Motosu Gun, Mino Province. Present Motosu City, Mizuho City, Kitagatacho in Gifu Prefecture. Later, the clan ruled over Mino Province and became Mino no kuninomiyatsuko.
