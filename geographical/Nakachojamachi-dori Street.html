Nakachojamachi-dori Street (中長者町通)

Nakachojamachi-dori Street is one of the streets running from east to west in Kamigyo Ward, Kyoto City. This street is located between Kamichojamachi-dori Street and Shimochojamachi-dori Street, and extends approx. 400m. The area along Nakachojamachi-dori Street which is located off the arterial highway and slightly in the west of the Kyoto Gyoen National Garden is a central part of an urban area where stores, offices and housings coexist.
