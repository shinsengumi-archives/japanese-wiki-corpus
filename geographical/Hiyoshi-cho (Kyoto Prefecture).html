Hiyoshi-cho (Kyoto Prefecture) (日吉町 (京都府))

Hiyoshi-cho was a town in Funai-gun, Kyoto Prefecture, which existed until December 31, 2005.

On January 1, 2006 four neighboring towns were merged to form Nantan City, thereby ending the history of Hiyoshi-cho as a local government. At present, Hiyoshi-cho remains an administrative district in Nantan City.

Geography
Hiyoshi-cho is located in the central part of Kyoto Prefecture.

Mountains: Mt.Kuroo and Mt.Omukai
Rivers: Kozumi-gawa River and Tahara-gawa River
