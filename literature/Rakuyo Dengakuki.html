Rakuyo Dengakuki (洛陽田楽記)

Rakuyo dengakuki is a book written by OE no Masafusa. This is a record of dengaku (ritual music and dancing in shrines and temples) in Kyoto in the summer of 1096, in the era of Emperor Horikawa, which is written in kanbun (Chinese classics). "Kojidan" (Talks of the Past) also contains articles of the same year.
