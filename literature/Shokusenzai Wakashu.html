Shokusenzai Wakashu (続千載和歌集)

Shokusenzai Wakashu (Waka Collection of a Thousand Years Continued) is the 15th imperial anthology of waka (Japanese poetry) by the command of the Cloistered Emperor Gouda in 1318. 20 volumes. The compiler was Tameyo NIJO. Main contributors include Tameuji NIJO, Sanekane SAIONJI, FUJIWARA no Tameie, and FUJIWARA no Sadaie. It consists of about 2100 poems.
