Marutamachi Station (丸太町駅)

Marutamachi stations are located in Kyoto City, Kyoto Prefecture. Two stations with the same name exist at different locations.

Marutamachi Station (Keihan) - Located in Sakyo Ward, it's a stop on the Keihan Oto Line. In the autumn of 2008, it is scheduled that the name of this station will be changed from Marutamachi Station to Jingu-marutamachi Station.

Marutamachi Station (Kyoto Municipal Subway) - Located in Nakagyo Ward, it's a stop on the Karasuma Line, which is operated by Kyoto Municipal Subway.
